Architecture Evolution: Sub-atomic Interconnects

Technical analysis of 2nm micro-architecture. In the current iteration of silicon design, electron tunneling has become the primary bottleneck. To counteract this, manufacturers are implementing liquid-state logic gates.

The L3 cache is now distributed across the entire die surface, creating a "neural mesh" of data. Any physical disturbance in the substrate leads to a cascade of register failures.

When the instruction pointer hits a corrupted address, the CPU enters a recursive loop of destruction. The hardware cannot recover from a logic-gate meltdown once the synchronization clock is lost.
